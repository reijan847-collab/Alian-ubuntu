Palawan – Famous for its crystal-clear waters, majestic limestone cliffs, and hidden lagoons. Ideal for snorkeling, kayaking, and discovering untouched natural beauty.
Boracay – Known for its powdery white sand, vibrant nightlife, and water sports. Perfect for sunbathing, island hopping, or enjoying fresh seafood by the shore.
Siargao – Surfing paradise with laid-back vibes, palm-fringed beaches, and lagoons. Great for surfing enthusiasts, exploring islands, and relaxing in a serene tropical setting.
